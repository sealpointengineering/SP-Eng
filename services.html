Independent Engineering Review & Design Certification

At SealPoint Engineering, we provide independent engineering reviews and professional certification services for clients who require an experienced Professional Engineer to thoroughly assess existing engineering designs developed by another firm or engineer.

Our review process goes beyond a surface-level check. We conduct a comprehensive technical evaluation of the engineering design to assess safety, code compliance, functionality, operability, and overall engineering integrity before authentication. Where appropriate, we provide professional stamping and certification after independently verifying that the design meets applicable engineering standards and regulatory requirements.

Our independent review services may include:

Design basis verification
Engineering calculation review
Code and standards compliance assessment
Mechanical and piping design evaluation
Pressure equipment and CRN support reviews
Safety and risk-focused engineering assessments
Material and specification verification
Constructability and operability review
Third-party engineering validation
Professional authentication and stamping

SealPoint Engineering works with manufacturers, engineering firms, fabricators, industrial facilities, and project owners who require an additional layer of technical assurance, independent verification, or professional authentication for engineering deliverables.

Every review is performed with independent professional judgment and in accordance with applicable engineering regulatory and ethical standards in Canada.
